Legal & Entity Structure

Our sophisticated, multi-entity structure ensures regulatory compliance, maximizes tax efficiency, minimizes investor liability, and clearly delineates roles for scalable growth.

Ceremonia Holdings, LLC

Provides consolidated governance and holds ownership stakes. Acts as the General Partner (GP) of the property-owning LLC.

Saint Vrain LandCo, LLC

The property owner and investment SPV. Fully insulated from direct operational risk. Provides fixed return to investors via the NNN lease.

Ceremonia Spaces, Inc. (OpCo)

The master tenant responsible for all operations and lease payments. Generates non-280E taxable income through room rentals.

Ceremonia Healing Center, Inc.

A separate C-Corp that holds the state healing center license, isolating all plant-touching activities and IRS §280E tax exposure.
