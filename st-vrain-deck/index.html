Recommendation Summary

This property offers an extremely compelling financial profile with a 4.16x DSCR that far exceeds Ceremonia's 1.5x target. The natural setting is arguably the best for healing center operations - the year-round creek, dramatic cliffs, and complete privacy on 12.82 acres create an irreplaceable therapeutic ambiance.

However, the investment hinges on two critical regulatory approvals: (1) septic system capacity must support 8-person occupancy without major upgrades, and (2) Boulder County Use Review must approve medical office use in residential zone. Either obstacle could add $50K-$100K in costs and 6-12 month delays.

Recommended Offer: $1,300,000-$1,325,000 with robust inspection contingencies and Use Review approval contingency. At $1,300,000, DSCR improves to 4.8x and cash-on-cash returns increase to 7.9%.
