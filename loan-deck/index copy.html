Market: A Specialty Medical Services Model

This business is structured as a specialty medical office facility (similar to an IPA or dental group practice). We provide licensed, compliant space for state-certified practitioners under Colorado’s Natural Medicine Health Act (NMHA) to serve clients. Ceremonia Spaces acts as the infrastructure provider in this new, regulated health services market.

About the Natural Medicine Health Act

Passed by Colorado voters in 2022 and implemented in 2025, the NMHA creates a regulated pathway for the therapeutic use of natural medicines. The Act requires:

  • Licensed Facilitators: Practitioners certified by the state after training and background checks.
  • Licensed Healing Centers: Approved facilities where natural medicine sessions must take place.
  • Compliance & Oversight: Regulated by Colorado’s Department of Regulatory Agencies (DORA).

Ceremonia Spaces provides compliant healing center facilities under this Act, positioning us as a first-mover in the Denver metro market.
