Key Research Findings
  • Johns Hopkins: Found psilocybin therapy's effect was four times larger than traditional antidepressants. At the 12-month mark, 58% of participants remained in remission from depression.
  • NYU: In a trial for Alcohol Use Disorder, participants receiving psilocybin therapy reduced their heavy drinking days by 83%, compared to a 51% reduction for those receiving an active placebo.

Your Questions Answered

We believe in transparency and want to address potential questions proactively with answers based on state regulations and established best practices.

We adhere to strict security protocols mandated by the Colorado Department of Revenue. All natural medicine is stored in a commercial-grade, bolted safe within a dedicated, locked room. In addition, every room where therapeutic sessions are conducted is equipped with security cameras as required by state regulation, with footage securely stored. Access is restricted to licensed, background-checked personnel.
Our screening process is extremely rigorous, modeled on clinical trial safety protocols. We exclude individuals with a history of psychosis or certain unstable medical conditions. Every participant undergoes a multi-session intake process to ensure they are a safe and appropriate candidate.
We carry comprehensive business insurance, including General Liability, Professional Liability, and Medical Malpractice insurance specifically designed for the natural medicine therapy industry, ensuring our facility, staff, and participants are fully protected.
The impact will be minimal. We operate by appointment only and host only one session per room per day. This means a very limited number of participants daily, resulting in a traffic impact significantly less than a typical doctor's office or retail business.
Compliance is the bedrock of our operation. We are dually licensed and regulated by two separate state agencies: DORA and the Department of Revenue. We are subject to regular inspections and must adhere to a comprehensive set of rules governing everything from security and inventory to participant safety.
